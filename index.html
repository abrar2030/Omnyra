ABOUT ME

Who I Am

1+ Years
Experience

I am a passionate Cloud & DevOps Engineer with over a year of experience designing, deploying, and automating cloud-native infrastructures using Kubernetes, CI/CD, containerization, and IaC (Terraform, Helm). I focus on streamlining workflows, optimizing deployments, and ensuring secure, high-performance environments.

At Ericsson, I automated infrastructure provisioning and deployment with Kubernetes, Docker, Helm, and CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins), improved cluster performance and observability with Prometheus and Grafana, and reinforced security with RBAC and network policies.

I have deployed AWS workloads using EKS, automated infrastructure with Terraform, and integrated storage and security measures, creating scalable, cost-effective multi-cloud architectures.

I enjoy collaborating with teams to adopt cloud-native technologies, refine CI/CD workflows, and implement Kubernetes best practices that boost software delivery and operational efficiency.

Holding a Computer Science degree from Eötvös Loránd University and solid real-world cloud automation experience, I am eager to take on a Junior to Medior Cloud/DevOps Engineer role that drives innovation and builds resilient infrastructures.
